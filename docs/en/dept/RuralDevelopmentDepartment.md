# Rural Development Department

**Date Range**: 30 March 2026 - 04 April 2026


## Funds
- According to the recommendations of the 15th Central Finance Commission, the Ministry of Panchayati Raj, Government of India had withheld funds of certain rural local self-government institutions where administrators were functioning. Among these, for those rural local self-government institutions where elections have been conducted and elected representatives have been appointed, an amount of 224.6514 crore, being the tied funds for the first and second installments for the year 2022 -23, is to be released/distributed.\
  [202603231215547520.pdf](https://gr.maharashtra.gov.in/Site/Upload/Government%20Resolutions/English/202603231215547520.pdf)

- According to the recommendations of the 15th Central Finance Commission, the Ministry of Panchayati Raj, Government of India had withheld funds of certain rural local self-government institutions due to the appointment of administrators. Among these, for those rural local self-government institutions where elections have been conducted and elected representatives have been appointed, the untied funds for the year 202324 amounting to 185.3252 crore (for the first and second installments) are to be released/distributed.\
